PAST PROJECTS

Relay for Life
Platform Building

The Team

A group of female students came in with different experiences and levels of expertise. Hack with Heart encouraged collaboration and innovative thinking to create a solution that positively and directly impacts an organization well known across the nation.

The Project

The project students built is a comprehensive platform informing all organizations and participants from Relay for Life of upcoming events and activities.
It supports all functions related to Relay for Life and provides an easily accessible method of accessing the specific details of all upcoming events while promoting communication between users through a message board feature. Furthermore, its simple, effective design allows for users to access a clear calendar and identify events and socials they wish to attend.

The Impact

Not only will this project benefit Relay for Life and contribute to the growth of their events campaign, but it will also promote their foundational principle, celebrating life and giving hope to those living with cancer.
